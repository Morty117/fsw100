Shake N Bake Origins

The origins of Shake N Bake began when Anthony met Katrisha and he began to support her dreams of becoming a baker for the people. Starting in their little 500 square foot apartment they began making pastries, boxed brownies, and other delicacies. Then they started experimenting and developing their own baking style and taste. Now they run this business out of their cottage-core home and right into yours.

Here at Shake N Bake we offer cakes, cupcakes and cakepops!
